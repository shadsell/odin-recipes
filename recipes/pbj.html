Peanut Butter and Jelly

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of white bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Strawberry jelly
  • Butter
  1. Spread peanut butter on a slice of bread
  2. Spread strawberry jelly on the other slice of bread
  3. Put the two slices together with the peanut butter and jelly on the inside
  4. Melt a pat of butter in a pan at medium heat
  5. Toast both sides of the sandwhich in the pan
  6. Take it out of the pan and cut diagonally

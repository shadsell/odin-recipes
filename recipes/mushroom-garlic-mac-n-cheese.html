Mushroom Garlic Mac n Cheese

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound pasta
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 16 oz portobello mushrooms, sliced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 1/2 cups gruyere cheese, shredded
  • 2 ounces goat cheese
  1. Cook pasta
  2. Melt half the butter in a saucepan
  3. Add garlic and mushrooms to butter and saute for 8 minutes
  4. Add salt and pepper
  5. Add remaining butter and stir until melted
  6. Add flour to saucepan, stir, and cook for a minute
  7. Add milk a little t a time, continuosly stirring until all milk is incorporated
  8. Cook stirring continuously for 5-8 minutes until the mixture simmers
  9. Remove sauce from heat
  10. Add gruyere and goat cheese. Stir until cheese melts
  11. Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss to combine

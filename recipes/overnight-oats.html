Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • a pinch of salt
  • a banana, sliced
  • 2 spoonfuls of peanut butter
  1. Mix all ingredients, except for the banana, together in a jar
  2. Screw the lid on then put in the fridge to sit overnight (at least 5 hours)
  3. In the morning, open the jar and add sliced banana
  4. Your oats are good in the fridge for 5 days
